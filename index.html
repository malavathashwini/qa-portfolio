About Me

Why I'm the Right Fit

A QA engineer who thinks like a user, tests like an engineer, and communicates like a professional.

My QA Philosophy

Quality is not an afterthought — it is built into every stage of the development lifecycle. I believe in proactive testing: catching defects early through meticulous test planning, thorough requirement analysis, and boundary-value thinking — long before a bug can reach a real user.


I pair technical rigour with clear, empathetic communication, ensuring developers, product managers, and stakeholders all share the same understanding of product quality at every sprint.

Core Strengths

  • Precision tester — I identify edge cases and boundary conditions that are commonly missed, reducing production incidents significantly.
  • Clear bug reporter — Every defect I log includes severity, priority, reproduction steps, expected vs actual results, and supporting evidence.
  • Fast domain learner — I ramp up quickly on new product domains and contribute to test planning from day one.
  • Collaborative partner — I bridge the gap between development and business to ensure quality is a shared team value, not a gatekeeper role.
  • Continuous improver — I actively pursue certifications and hands-on practice to stay current with QA best practices and tooling.
